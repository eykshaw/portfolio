Student Apartment Project

2016 | AutoCAD

The student apartment project was the final project from Design 21, Drafting and Perspective. For this project, I wanted to emphasize affordable housing for students. The layout of fairly open between the public and private spaces because I want to encourage social activites and transparency between students. Transparency is important in all relationships, and I believe breaking down the literal walls between students will help to form long lasting relationships into the rest of their lives.

The apartment follows the standard layout of a studio apartment, wherein the bedroom and the living and kitchen area all occupy the same space. The bedroom area can be separated by the folding partition, and further distinction between the public and private spaces is made by the drop ceiling over the sleeping area.

For this project, I built a scale model at 1" = 1', which is shown below. Light, pastel colors were chosen to align to the minimalist layout of the apartment and are accented by the bold red patterned backsplash in the kitchen, a place that is generally the central social area for college students.
