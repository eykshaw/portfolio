A poster of the character Cookie Cat from Cartoon Network's Steven Universe. The lively colors and the fun art style are meant to fall in line with the feel of the show, as Steven Universe is mainly a fun, lighthearted cartoon for children and is animated in a very cartoony style.

2016 | Adobe Illustrator

A poster of the character Amethyst from Cartoon Network's Steven Universe. The colors for this poster are darker than that of the Cookie Cat poster, because while Amethyst often acts as the comic relief character, she has deep seeded self-doubt issues that make her character a bit darker than Cookie Cat. She's a very physically active character, so I chose to portray her mid-leap.
